# 🚀 Manzil AI

## 📌 About

**Manzil AI** is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed to help students choose the right career path according to their interests, skills, and abilities.

Many students face confusion after 10th and 12th while selecting a career. Manzil AI solves this problem by providing personalized career recommendations using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 🎯 How It Works

Manzil AI asks students **20 special career assessment questions** based on their interests, skills, and preferences.

After analyzing the responses using AI, the platform suggests the most suitable career fields and provides guidance to help students make better decisions.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Career Assistant
An AI-based assistant that helps students with career-related doubts and provides guidance for their future decisions.

### 📝 20 Special Career Questions
A personalized assessment system where students answer 20 questions. AI analyzes their answers and suggests the best career options according to their talent and interests.

### 🛣️ Career Roadmap
Provides a complete step-by-step roadmap from starting point to career goal, making the journey easier for students.

### 📅 Daily Tasks
Gives students daily tasks that help them continuously improve and move closer towards their career goals.

### ⚔️ Career Battle
Helps students compare two career options by explaining their benefits, scope, and recommends the better choice.

### 🔮 Career Prediction
Analyzes future career trends and informs students about careers with better future opportunities.

### 👨‍🏫 AI Mentor (Pandu Ram)
An AI mentor named **Pandu Ram** that helps students in making career decisions and provides study-related guidance.

### 👨‍👩‍👦 Parents Dashboard
A dedicated dashboard where parents can track their child's selected career path and progress.

### 💬 Parent Explanation Message
AI generates easy-to-understand messages to help students explain their career choices to their parents.

### 🌎 Career Explore
Students can explore different career options and get detailed information about each field.

### 🎥 AI Career Videos
For every career option, AI generates simple explanation videos that describe:
- What the career is
- How it works
- Required skills
- Future scope
- Career opportunities

### 🌐 Multi-Language Support
Provides career guidance in multiple languages so students can understand easily.
